Welcome to the new Schedule App!

There are several tools at Rose-Hulman that present data in different ways. For example, the schedule lookup page, banner web, course catalogs, and moodle have data on students, faculty, courses, terms, and schedule. However, all the data is not collocated; thus it is challenging to perform simple analyses on the data and glean simple information from the collective dataset.

The goal of this website is to bring all this data together into one collective dataset and give a user (e.g., department head, faculty, advisor, student) the ability to perform simple queries/analyses and glean important information from the collective dataset.

To begin, simply view the side nagivagtion bar to go to view a list of your classes, create a group for one of the terms, and much more. Along with the side navigation bar, one can simply enter in the appropriate information below to search for specific details about a specific term.
