Welcome to the new Schedule App!

There are several tools at Rose-Hulman that present data in different ways. For example, the schedule lookup page, banner web, course catalogs, and moodle have data on students, faculty, courses, terms, and schedule. However, all the data is not collocated; thus it is challenging to perform simple analyses on the data and glean simple information from the collective dataset. This website brings all that data together into one collective dataset and give a user (e.g., department head, faculty, advisor, student) the ability to perform simple queries/analyses and glean important information from the collective dataset.

Only active Rose-Hulman students and faculty will have access to this site.
